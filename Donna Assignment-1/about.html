The Art Gallery of Colour, Shape and Style opened in August 2018.

Located at 1234 Art Nouveau Blvd. in Toronto's downtown core, we have established ourselves as Canada's first and foremost exhibitor of all things colourful. Shades and tints abound in our exhibits, our colour palette consistently impresses and our monochromatic galleria soothes the senses. See our amazing colour wheel in the rotunda. Our primary and secondary colours are second to none! Visit our Tertiary Garden and relax in our forest of analogous colours and vector shapes.
